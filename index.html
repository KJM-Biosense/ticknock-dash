Fossitt classification
i
Fossitt is the standard Irish habitat classification system. Each code represents a distinct habitat type.
Artificial vs semi-natural
i
Semi-natural habitats have developed with minimal human modification. Artificial habitats are heavily modified, such as conifer plantations.
Mosaic habitats
i
A mosaic habitat is where two or more habitat types occur together in a patch too small to map separately.
